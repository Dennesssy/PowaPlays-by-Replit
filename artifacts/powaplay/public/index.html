POWAPLAY

by Replit

Why I Built This

During Replit's Agent 4 Buildathon, thousands of builders shipped incredible apps in record time. But after the buildathon ended, those projects were scattered — hard to find, easy to forget. I wanted a place where every project could live on, be discovered, and get the recognition it deserves.

POWAPLAY was born from that need: a living gallery of real apps built by real people on Replit. Not mockups. Not demos. Real, working software you can explore with a tap.

What It Is Now

A pannable marketplace of 1,300+ projects synced directly from Replit buildathons. Every tile is a real app you can open, try, and favorite. Filter by category, search by name, and discover projects you never knew existed.

Behind the scenes, there's a full enterprise observability stack — sync health monitoring, APM metrics, alerting, and an admin audit trail — ensuring the platform stays fast and reliable.

What It Could Be

Imagine a decentralized app store where any Replit developer can publish, get feedback, and grow their user base. Curated collections, trending projects, builder profiles, real-time collaboration reviews — a platform where the best Replit apps surface organically.

POWAPLAY could become the place builders go to launch, and users go to discover the next great tool, game, or utility — all built on Replit's infrastructure.
